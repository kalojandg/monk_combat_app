Resurrection — Racial & Dragonmark Traits
[Dragonmark] Mark of Shadow

You bear the Mark of Shadow — a dragonmark of illusion and stealth. As you are not a full elf, only the partial manifestation is available to you.

Cunning Intuition

When you make a Charisma (Performance) or Dexterity (Stealth) check, you can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to the ability check.

Shape Shadows

You know the minor illusion cantrip.

Starting at 3rd level, you can cast invisibility once with this trait, and you regain the ability to cast it when you finish a long rest. Charisma is your spellcasting ability for these spells.

Spells of the Mark

If you have the Spellcasting or the Pact Magic class feature, the following spells are added to the spell list of your spellcasting class.

• 1st: disguise self, silent image
• 2nd: darkness, pass without trace
• 3rd: clairvoyance, major image
• 4th: greater invisibility, hallucinatory terrain
• 5th: mislead
