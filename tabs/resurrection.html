Resurrection — Racial & Dragonmark Traits
[Dragonmark] Mark of Shadow

You bear the Mark of Shadow — a dragonmark of illusion and stealth. As you are not a full elf, only the partial manifestation is available to you.

Keen Senses. You have proficiency in the Perception skill.

Cunning Intuition. When you make a Charisma (Performance) or Dexterity (Stealth) check, you can roll a d4 and add the number rolled to the ability check.
